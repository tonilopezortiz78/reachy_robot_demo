## Network School (ns.com)

**What it is:** A residential "startup society" founded by Balaji Srinivasan, located in Forest City, Malaysia — 20 minutes from Singapore in the Johor Special Economic Zone. Operationalises Balaji's thesis from his book *The Network State*: internet communities can become sovereign physical societies.

**Founded:** September 2024. Now 2,000+ members across 16+ cohorts. 80+ nationalities represented.

**Cost:** ~$1,500/month all-in — housing, food, gym, coworking.

**Founder:** Balaji Srinivasan — former CTO of Coinbase, former General Partner at a16z, author of *The Network State*.

### Core Framework: Learn. Burn. Earn. Fun.

| Pillar | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Learn** | Workshops, founder talks, proof-of-learn NFT credentials in tech, AI, crypto, humanities |
| **Burn** | Daily gym, structured fitness, longevity nutrition (Bryan Johnson-style) |
| **Earn** | Crypto bounties, real paid tasks (dev, design, content), career and funding office hours |
| **Fun** | College-town community, positive-sum high-agency people, everyone levelling up together |

### Core Values & Principles

- **Techno-optimism** — technology solves problems, build the future instead of complaining about it
- **Decentralisation** — money, identity, governance should all be decentralised
- **Meritocracy** — global meritocracy is here; merit over geography or credentials
- **Sovereignty** — individuals and communities should be free from legacy government structures
- **Internationalism** — 80+ countries, deliberately recruits underrepresented global talent
- **Builder culture** — "Come to build the next Harvard, not just attend it"

### Who Attends

Tech founders, AI builders, crypto enthusiasts, digital nomads, remote workers, community organisers. ~50% aspiring builders, ~25% actively building startups, ~25% freelancers and nomads.

### Mission Statement

> "Turn internet communities into physical startup societies."
> "Materialising the cloud upon the land."

### Long-Term Vision

- **v3** — Permanent campus
- **v4** — Open-source blueprint for global replication
- **v5** — NS graduates founding their own network states

### Notable Facts

- Over 4,000 applications from 80+ countries for the first cohort
- Backed by Balaji Fund — investors include Brian Armstrong, Naval Ravikant, Fred Wilson
- Malaysian government engaging NS for broader Johor economic zone cooperation
